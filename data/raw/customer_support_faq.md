# Customer Support FAQ

## Refund Status

Support agents may provide refund status updates when the customer provides an order ID and the email address used for the purchase. If either value is missing, the agent should ask for the missing information before checking the case.

Refunds approved by the support team are usually processed within 5 business days. Bank processing time may add 2 to 3 additional business days.

## Required Information for Refund Questions

For refund-related questions, support agents should collect:

- Order ID
- Purchase email address
- Purchase date
- Reason for refund
- Product condition

If the customer reports a damaged or defective product, the agent should also request a short description of the issue and supporting photos when available.

## Claim Document Questions

For claim-related questions, support agents should ask for the claim reference number before discussing case status. Agents should not request sensitive personal data in chat unless the approved secure intake form is being used.

## Processing Times

Standard support requests should receive an initial response within 2 business days. Escalated compliance cases may take up to 5 business days for review.

## Contact Channels

Customers may contact support through email, live chat, or the support portal. Compliance escalations must be handled through the support portal so that all required review fields are captured.
