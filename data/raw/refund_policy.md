# Refund Policy

## Standard Refund Window

Customers may request a refund within 14 calendar days of purchase if the item is unused, the original receipt is available, and the product was purchased through an approved sales channel.

Refunds requested within the standard window may be approved by a support agent if all required information is present and there are no fraud or compliance indicators.

## Refunds After 14 Days

Refunds after 14 calendar days require manager approval unless the product is defective or the delay was caused by a verified shipping issue.

Support agents should not promise approval for late refund requests. They may explain the policy and collect information for manager review.

## Damaged or Defective Products

If a customer reports a damaged or defective product, the support agent should collect:

- Order ID
- Purchase date
- Description of the defect
- Photos or other supporting evidence
- Whether the product has been used

Defective product cases may be eligible for refund, replacement, or repair depending on the product category and evidence provided.

## Missing Receipt

If the customer cannot provide a receipt, the support agent may use the order ID and purchase email address to verify the transaction. If the purchase cannot be verified, the case must be escalated to a manager.

## Non-Refundable Items

The following items are normally non-refundable:

- Digital products after access has been granted
- Gift cards
- Items marked final sale
- Products damaged by misuse

Exceptions require manager approval.

## Fraud or Compliance Concerns

Support agents must escalate refund requests when there are fraud indicators, repeated high-value refund requests, inconsistent customer information, or requests involving restricted products.
